about us

✔️Welcome to Geurin restaurant, your go-to destination for fresh, flavorful, and artfully crafted sushi. Founded with a deep appreciation for Japanese culinary tradition, our restaurant brings together authentic taste, quality ingredients, and a cozy, modern dining experience. At the heart of our menu are our signature rolls — carefully prepared to delight both newcomers and sushi enthusiasts alike. Try the timeless California Roll, with its perfect balance of crab stick, avocado, and cucumber, all wrapped in seasoned rice and nori. Indulge in the creamy, rich flavor of our Salmon Mentai, topped with our special mentai sauce and lightly torched to bring out its smoky umami notes. Savor the subtle sweetness of our Crab Stick Roll, or turn up the heat with the spicy, creamy explosion of our Dynamite Roll — a favorite for those who like bold flavors.

✔️We believe that sushi is not just food, but an experience — one that celebrates freshness, balance, and the beauty of simplicity. That’s why we take great care in selecting only the freshest ingredients, from high-quality seafood to premium rice and house-made sauces. Every roll is handcrafted with precision and passion by our skilled chefs, ensuring consistency, taste, and satisfaction in every bite. Our restaurant is designed to make you feel right at home. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a casual dinner with friends, or a special occasion, we offer a warm and inviting space where good food and good company come together. We also offer takeout and delivery options, so you can enjoy your favorite rolls wherever you are. Thank you for choosing Geurin. We are proud to serve you, and we look forward to sharing the joy of sushi with you — one roll at a time. ________________________________________
